Welcome to the Surah Progress Tracking Site


Instructions on how to use the site:

Select the Surah you want to read and the language you would like the Surah to be translated to!

Click Find Surah to view the Surah selected with the Surah and Language of your choice

Click View Previous Saved Progress to review your previous saved progress

You can either start reading from the first Surah to the last Surah and take note of your progress here
(the dropdown list has arranged the chapters of the Al-Quran chronologically etc.. "1 Al Fatihaa" means it is the first Surah in the Al-Quran)
Or you can memorise a particular Surah and take note of your progress here
